Tackling Urban Flood

The Problem

Flash Flood - A problem affecting millions of lives

What?
Urban Flash Flood

Bangladesh being a very wet country sees on average about 2,200 millimeters (mm) of rainfall per year. This heavy rainfall causes flooding especially on the monsoon season. The main victims are the people living in unplanned urban areas.

Why?
A Tale of Poor Management

The densely populated cities are built on poor sewage management system having little to no synchronization among the regulatory bodies. As a result, solid waste and debries clogged inside of the drains restricts the natural flow of the system.

How?
Technology to Tackle Flood

We are on a mission with our Project FloodNot to unlock this gridlock leveraging state-of-the-art technology. We devised a solution combining 5G, Cloud and IoT technologies to make lives cleaner, healthier and safer.
